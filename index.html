My Inspiration

Kama is a fun loving and up for anything kind of woman. She loves a good adventure and anything to do with a new experience or outdoors. She was raised by two parents who helped to instill good values in her including family. While still being very family oriented, she still loves to explore things on her own. It is a sort of free spirit personality that she has and she often times doesn’t worry about the small things.

She is motivated by family and that includes her husband and new addition, an 8-month baby boy. Becoming a new mom has been an incredible experience for her and she truly is a great mom and provides her son with lots of new experiences. She found her passion for kids early on in her 20’s and she still continues to light that fire every day for her students. I could get a sense of her passion for kids and teaching when she talks about it and her kids. She says it is always bittersweet to have to lose a class to a new grade, but she knows they are ready when she is done and that she is always proud when she hears good words about her students from the previous years.
