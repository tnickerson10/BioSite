About Me

Kama Bergeron Nickerson was born and raised in Council Bluffs, Iowa with two brothers and a sister to round out the family. Growing up she loved to be outdoors and played just about every sport there is at least once, but it was soccer, volleyball and tennis that she played in High School. She grew up always traveling and her family had a house in the Ozarks where they loved to enjoy the lake life. Boating was her favorite of them all. It was the cool breeze and fresh air that made her the happiest.

After graduating High School, Kama attended the University of Nebraska at Lincoln where she obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology. Shortly after she decided to move to San Diego Ca. to go get her teaching credential at Point Loma Nazarene where she obtained her K-5 and special education credential. While teaching at her first job, Excelsior Academy a small Non-Profit school for special needs kids she met her now husband. In addition, she is now a proud mom of a happy and healthy baby boy that is 8 months old. The zoo is her favorite place to go with him and he loves the aquarium the most.
